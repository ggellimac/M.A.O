A Great Starting Point

Movement As One Dance Company is an awesome opportunity for those of you who have been interested in dancing, but have no prior dance experience. Here at M.A.O, we take pride in our dance classes that feature both choreography and fundamental concepts that attract both new and advanced dancers alike. As of now, our main focus is urban style dancing or "hip-hop", however we do occasionally have classes that range from contemporary and breaking to heels and locking.s

group

Welcoming Environment

As part of our mission, we believe in promoting a positve and encouraging social and learning atmosphere in order to facilitate as much growth as possible for our dancers. Our values as a dance company extend past the classroom as we motivate our members to be a part of this family by forming new bonds with each other through dancing, social events, and community service.

settings

Dedicated to the Community

Our members are comprised mainly of college students throughout the Atlanta and Kennesaw area. Our classes and workshops take place at convenient times that work around a typical college student schedule, and our member dues are priced at an affordable cost. However, you do NOT have to be a college student to join M.A.O! Quite a few of our members are also full-time employees who have taken an interest in learning dance.

MAOtimedia

Our MAOtimedia committee specializes in showcasing our team's greatest moments and hits. Like all of our committees, any M.A.O member is welcome to volunteer and gain valuable multimedia experience. Subscribe to our YouTube channel to keep up with our latest videos, and like our Facebook page to see event updates and photo albums!

M.A.O Gear

Support M.A.O by rocking our team's merch! Each year, we release a new shirt design to commemorate the beginning of a new season. Carefully designed by our Creative Director, our team shirts are sold for a limited amount of time before the next season begins.
